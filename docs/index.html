Idea and the Format

Earth systems are complex. Most of the newer methods in AI are aimed at the challenges in the field of computer vision and natural language processing. There are similarities and dissimilarities of these challenges with the challenges in understanding the earth systems. The idea is to bridge the gap and have an understanding about how newer methods can be modified/transferred to solve the challenges of Earth Systems.

AIM4ES Seminars are held on the 1st and the 3rd Monday of every month where AI methods related papers are discussed and we try to understand how this can be used to understand the problems in the Earth Systems. The 1 hour seminar begins with a 15 minutes introduction of the paper (through slides/ documents) to initiate a discussion. The discussion is planned for 35 minutes followed by a 5 minute wrap-up and last 5 minutes for suggestions and feeedbacks.
